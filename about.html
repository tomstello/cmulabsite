About Tom

Dr. Tom Costello

Assistant Professor, Carnegie Mellon University

Founder, AI Persuasion & Truth Lab

Dr. Tom Costello is an Assistant Professor at Carnegie Mellon University and the founder of the AI Persuasion & Truth Lab. His research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, persuasion, and truth, exploring how AI can be used to promote truth and combat misinformation. Dr. Costello's work has been recognized with numerous awards, including the National Science Foundation CAREER Award and the Association for Computing Machinery Doctoral Dissertation Award. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University and a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of California, Berkeley.
