Join the Lab

Graduate Students

We are always looking for passionate and motivated graduate students to join our team. If you are interested in the intersection of AI, persuasion, and truth, and have a strong background in computer science, psychology, or a related field, we encourage you to apply to the Ph.D. program at Carnegie Mellon University. Please mention your interest in our lab in your application and feel free to reach out to Dr. Costello directly with your CV and a brief statement of research interests.

Postdoctoral Researchers

We are seeking talented postdoctoral researchers to contribute to our ongoing projects. Ideal candidates will have a Ph.D. in a relevant field and a proven track record of research excellence. If you are interested in a postdoctoral position, please email Dr. Costello with your CV, a statement of research interests, and contact information for three references.

Undergraduate Students

We have opportunities for undergraduate students at Carnegie Mellon University to get involved in our research. If you are an undergraduate student with a strong academic record and a passion for our research areas, please email Dr. Costello with your resume and a brief statement of interest to inquire about available positions.
