AI Persuasion & Truth Lab

The AI Persuasion & Truth Lab at Carnegie Mellon University, led by Dr. Tom Costello, explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, persuasion, and truth. Our mission is to develop AI systems that can effectively communicate and persuade while upholding principles of truthfulness and ethical conduct. We investigate how AI can be used to promote understanding, facilitate informed decision-making, and combat misinformation. Our research spans various domains, including health communication, political discourse, and educational technologies.

Our Lab Culture

Our lab fosters a collaborative and inclusive environment where researchers from diverse backgrounds can thrive. We value intellectual curiosity, rigorous research, and a commitment to making a positive impact on society. We regularly engage in team-building activities, workshops, and social events to strengthen our community and support each other's growth.
